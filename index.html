Bread, In All Of Its Glory!

Introduction

Bread has long been enjoyed by the people of this planet. Praised for its nutritional value and flavor, this website hopes to further celebrate this key part of society. Bread is traditionally made with just flour, water, and leavening, but many more elaborate types of bread have been created over the centuries.

Varieties of bread

Type of Bread Unique elements Location of Origin
Baguette Elongated shape France
Sourdough Naturally leavened with unique flavor Largely unknown,possibly Egypt or the Fertile Cresant
Rye Bread Made with rye flour, giving it a darker color Europe, likely Germany
Bagel Torus Shaped, chewy and dense interior Jewish communities of Poland
Ciabatta Long, flat shape with an open crumb Italy
Naan Soft, chewy flatbread Central, West, and Southeast Asia
